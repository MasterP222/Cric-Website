My Experience

What Happened

I started this Bootcamp to teach cricket in a fun way. At first, it was hard to get people to come. Parents don't want someone inexperienced teaching their children. I had to spend a lot of time marketing: showing my highlights and convincing them I deserve to teach. On the first day, I had only 2 students, and I did my best to teach them the basics. They liked it and they invited their friends. I soon had 6 students, and although it was tough, I did my best to make it enjoyable and also be informative. Those 3 weeks were fun, not just for the students but for me too. And seeing them smile and be happy was the best feeling in the world.
